YOU'RE WELCOME HERE!

SERVICE TIMES: SUNDAYS AT 8AM - 11AM

LOCATION: 16B IDIROKO ORANFE ILE-IFE, OSUN STATE, NIGERIA

At Christ Life For The World Ministry, Sunday is more than a service, it's a family gathering where everyone belongs. Experience uplifting worship, practical teaching, and quality programs for kids, all in a welcoming environment where you can be yourself. We'd love to get to know you, so come this Sunday and see what makes our community special!

WHAT TO EXPECT:

  • A vibrant service that typically lasts 3 – 4 hours
  • Inspiring, Bible-based messages that encourage and challenge you
  • Practical teaching from our Pastor to guide everyday life
  • Authentic worship that draws you closer to God
  • Fun, safe, and engaging programs designed just for your kids

FOR THE KIDS

Every Sunday, children through primary school discover who God is and who they are in Him through age-appropriate lessons, small groups, and caring adult leaders. Our goal is to create a safe, secure, and fun environment where kids can grow in their faith, build lasting friendships, and enjoy an exciting church experience made just for them.

FOR THE TEENS

Every third Saturday of every month Christ Life For The World Ministry Secondary School Students (Teenagers) meet. Loaded with interactive activities, and small group time it addresses real issues teens face each day. Our major push for the secondary school years is to equip our students to face the challenges that will come their way, as they venture out into a world that will test their faith.

OUR VISION

To raise a Christ-centered people who transfoem lives, families, and nations by the power of the Gospel
