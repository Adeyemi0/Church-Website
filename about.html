About The Church

CHRIST CENTERED
TRANSFORMATION

Christ Life For The World Ministry is a mission-minded teaching ministry founded on 10th of December 2016. It is dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through the power of God's Word.

Our Vision

To raise a christ Centered people who transform lives, families, and nations by the power of the Gospel

Our Mission

Our mission is to preach Christ and the gospel of His kingdom, making His message known and relevant in every aspect of life. We are committed to discipling believers into spiritual maturity, helping them grow deeper in their faith and walk with God.

We also seek to serve our community in love and truth, reflecting Christ’s character through compassion and integrity. In doing so, we prepare the saints for the coming of the Lord, equipping them to live with purpose and readiness for His return.

Our Core Values

The Word of God

We hold firmly to the Bible as God's inspired, inerrant Word, the final authority for doctrine, faith, and practice. We are committed to teaching, studying, and applying its truths for transformed living.

Scripture: "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work." (2 Timothy 3:16-17)

Prayer & Worship

We prioritize fervent, constant prayer as the foundation of our ministry and a direct connection to God. We embrace authentic, Spirit-led worship as our highest response to God's presence and greatness.

Scripture: "Rejoice always, pray continually, give thanks in all circumstances; for this is God’s will for you in Christ Jesus." (1 Thessalonians 5:16-18)

Holiness & Integrity

As Christ's followers, we are called to a life of purity, consecration, and moral excellence. We are committed to reflecting God’s character in our personal and public lives with integrity and truth.

Scripture: "But just as he who called you is holy, so be holy in all you do; for it is written: 'Be holy, because I am holy.'" (1 Peter 1:15-16)

Love & Compassion

We are devoted to expressing Christ's unconditional love and compassion to all people. We foster a vibrant, welcoming community where believers are united in fellowship, encouragement, and mutual care.

Scripture: "A new command I give you: Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another. By this everyone will know that you are my disciples, if you love one another." (John 13:34-35)

Evangelism & Service

We are obedient to the Great Commission, actively sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ locally and globally. We express our faith through practical, compassionate service to meet the needs of our community and the world.

Scripture: "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you." (Matthew 28:19-20)

Empowerment by the Spirit

We believe in the active presence and power of the Holy Spirit, who indwells, empowers, and guides believers. We rely on the Spirit to manifest His gifts for the edification of the Church and to accomplish our mission.

Scripture: "But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth." (Acts 1:8)

WE BELIEVE

  • In one God—Who expresses Himself as the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.
  • That the Bible is God’s inspired Word and the final authority for faith and conduct.
  • In the virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.
  • That salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • In the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the operation of spiritual gifts.
  • In water baptism and the Lord’s Supper as ordinances of the church.
  • In the second coming of Christ and eternal life for all believers.

Meet Our Pastors

Pastor (Dr.) Banjo Oluseyi O.

Senior Pastor & Founder

Dr. Banjo Oluseyi is a passionate and gifted preacher of God's Word, Pastor Oluseyi O. is a visionary leader with over 15 years of ministry experience. Called to ministry at a young age, he has dedicated his life to the preaching of the gospel of the kingdom of God and raising a generation of believers who walk in the fullness of their identity.

His teaching ministry is marked by exceptional clarity, depth, and practical application of biblical truth. A gifted radio evangelist, with undeniable testimonies of healing and deliverance. His unwavering commitment to excellence has positioned Christ Life For The World Ministry as a beacon of light in the community.

Dr. Banjo Olufunmilayo Olufunmilola

Co-Pastor & Women’s Ministry Leader

Dr. Olufunmilayo is a woman of grace, wisdom, and unwavering faith. Alongside her husband, she ministers with a heart dedicated to seeing families restored and women empowered. Her impactful ministry to women has transformed countless lives, bringing healing and alignment with their God-given purpose.

Together, Pastor Oluseyi and Dr. Olufunmilayo exemplify a Christ-centered marriage and partnership in ministry. They are blessed with children who share their passion for God’s Kingdom. Their combined leadership has fostered a church culture of love, excellence, and kingdom impact.
